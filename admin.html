Syllabus: The course is intended for biology graduate students interested in extending their knowledge and skills in programming and computational biology. The course will combine practical programming skills in the programming language Python with computational modeling and analysis of biological data. For example, students will do sequence data analysis, mathematical modeling of biological processes, processing of biological images, statistical analysis and visualization of experimental and observational results.
Prerequisites: None
Grade composition: 50% exam, 50% project, presentation and homework.
