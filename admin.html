Sylabus

The course presents programming principles in Python. The course mainly deals with the applicative aspects of programming and students will acquire basic programming skills. The course deals with general topics: Python programming language, use of external libraries, recursion, runtime analysis of sorting algorithms, dynamic programming, exception handling, IO and more. On the applicative side, the course will present applications from different fields of engineering and computer science: simulation, optimization, data analysis, signal processing, GUI and more.
Requirements: None.
Exam dates: TBD.

Grades

Exercises: there will be 10 exercises during the course and students will be required to submit and pass (grade >= 60) at least 9 exercises in order to complete the course. Grades will be 0, 60, 80, 90 or 100. All exercises will have the same weight for the final grade.
Exam: in order to complete the course students must pass the exam (grade >= 60).
Final grade: The exercises grade will be 20-30% of the final grade.
Questions: questions regarding course material and homewok should be posted to the forum. When emailing with personal issues, please email only one staff member.
